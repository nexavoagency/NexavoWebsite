FAQ

How long does a typical project take? ▼

It depends on scope. A basic website takes 3–7 days. A complex e-commerce or ERP project can take 2–6 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

Do you offer post-delivery support? ▼

Yes. We provide 30 days of free support after delivery for bug fixes. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages for continuous support.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept bank transfer, PayPal, and other arrangements based on location. We typically require 50% upfront and 50% on delivery.

Can you work with international clients? ▼

Absolutely. We work with clients worldwide. Our team is available for meetings via video call in most time zones.

Do you sign NDAs? ▼

Yes, we are happy to sign NDAs before any project discussion. Client confidentiality is very important to us.
